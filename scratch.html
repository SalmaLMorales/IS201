The Language I Was Never Taught - But Always Needed

Learning Kichwa started as a hobby, but it has become one of my biggest passions. It's a journey of growth, healing, and connection to my roots.

  1. Why I Chose Kichwa as My Hobby?

    • It brings me closer to my Indigenous identity
    • I love learning new words and sounds
    • It is not only about the language but also about the traditions I learn

  2. Challenges I Have Faced Learning Kichwa

    • I didn’t grow up speaking it
    • There are limited resources and teachers
    • People in Ecuador don't appreciate this beautiful language

  3. What I’m Proud of

    • I can understand some conversations now
    • I’ve memorized some traditional phrases and prayers
    • I am part of a small learning group that meets every month

  4. What I Want You to Know

    • Kichwa is more than just a language—it’s a way of seeing the world
    • Supporting language preservation helps protect cultures
    • You don’t have to be fluent to be part of the movement—just curious and respectful
